
# Blue Room


How many days have you been stuck in this two-room prison? You don't recall. The days blend into each other like milk and coffee, not that you know what those things are (Your only form of sustenence is the thick, grimey nutritional paste dispensed after you completed your daily tasks, which you hate.) Find a way out, or wallow in a never-ending monotonous nightmare. 

A short puzzle game about a post-dystopian future originally created for a class project inspired by the short story, "I Have No Mouth and I Must Scream." 


## Controls

- Mouse - Look
- Mouse1 - Interact (Computer)
- E - Interact (Environment)
- ESC - Exit/Pause

## Your Daily Tasks
- Solve your daily CAPTCHAs!
- Eat the tasty nutritional paste provided.
- Meditate and relax.
- Sleep for 8 hours.
